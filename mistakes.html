Common Beginner Trading Mistakes

Trading Without Learning First

Many beginners start using real money before understanding risk, charts, or trading plans.

Using Money Needed for Bills

Never trade rent money, food money, car payment money, or emergency money.

Following Hype

Do not buy a stock just because social media says it is going up.

No Exit Plan

Before buying, know when you would sell for a profit and when you would sell to stop a loss.

Revenge Trading

Revenge trading means making another trade quickly because you are upset about losing. This can cause bigger losses.
