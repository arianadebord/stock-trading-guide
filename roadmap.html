Beginner Trading Roadmap

Follow the Path

This roadmap helps beginners learn in order instead of jumping straight into real-money trading.

1. Learn Stock Basics

Understand stocks, shares, brokers, markets, and trading platforms.

2. Learn Chart Reading

Study price lines, candlesticks, support, resistance, volume, and trends.

3. Learn Risk Management

Understand stop losses, position sizing, and why beginners should risk very little.

4. Learn Trading Psychology

Study fear, greed, FOMO, revenge trading, patience, and discipline.

5. Build a Watchlist

Pick stocks to study before buying. Do not rush into trades.

6. Practice With Paper Trading

Use fake money to practice entries, exits, and emotions.

7. Keep a Trading Journal

Write down every practice trade and what you learned.

8. Create a Trading Plan

Decide your rules before entering trades.

9. Complete the Paper Trading Challenge

Try to complete 50 paper trades while following your rules.

10. Review Before Real Money

Only consider real money after practicing, journaling, and understanding the risks.
