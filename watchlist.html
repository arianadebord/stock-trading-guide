Building a Beginner Watchlist

What Is a Watchlist?

A watchlist is a list of stocks you want to study before buying. It helps you slow down and learn instead of rushing into trades.

What To Add

Beginners can add companies they understand, popular ETFs, or stocks they want to research more. Do not buy just because a stock is popular.

What To Track

Track the stock name, ticker symbol, current price, why you are watching it, support area, resistance area, and any important news.

Beginner Watchlist Example

Ticker: AAPL

Reason: Popular company, easy to research, lots of news available.

Plan: Watch the chart and practice with fake money first.

Watchlist Rules

☐ I know what the company does.

☐ I checked recent news.

☐ I looked at the chart.

☐ I am practicing before using real money.

☐ I am not buying because of hype.
