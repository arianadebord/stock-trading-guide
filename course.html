Beginner Trading Course

Module 1: Stock Market Basics

Learn what stocks are, what shares are, what brokers do, and how buying and selling works.

Module 2: Chart Reading

Learn price lines, candlesticks, support, resistance, volume, and trends.

Module 3: Risk Management

Learn how to protect your account, use stop losses, avoid risking too much, and practice with fake money first.

Module 4: Trading Psychology

Learn about FOMO, greed, fear, revenge trading, patience, and discipline.

Module 5: Paper Trading

Practice trades without using real money. Track what you did, why you did it, and what you learned.

Module 6: Watchlists

Build a list of stocks to study. Learn how to research before making decisions.

Module 7: Trading Plan

Create rules for entries, exits, risk, goals, and what to do after wins or losses.

Final Beginner Goal

Before using real money, complete at least 50 paper trades and review your journal.
