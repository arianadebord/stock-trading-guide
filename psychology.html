Trading Psychology

Education Only

This is not financial advice. Trading can lose money. Practice first.

Why Psychology Matters

Many beginners do not lose because they are dumb. They lose because emotions take control. Fear, greed, impatience, and FOMO can make people enter bad trades.

FOMO

FOMO means fear of missing out. It happens when you buy only because a stock is moving fast or people online are talking about it.

Rule: If you missed the move, wait for another setup.

Revenge Trading

Revenge trading is when you lose money and immediately make another trade to try to win it back. This usually causes bigger losses.

Rule: After a loss, stop and review your plan.

Greed

Greed makes traders hold too long because they want more profit. Sometimes a winning trade turns into a losing trade.

Rule: Decide your exit before entering.

Fear

Fear can make beginners sell too early, avoid good practice setups, or panic when the price moves normally.

Rule: Use paper trading until you feel calmer following a plan.

Beginner Mindset Checklist

☐ I am not rushing.

☐ I know why I am entering.

☐ I know where I would exit.

☐ I accept that losses happen.

☐ I am practicing before using real money.
