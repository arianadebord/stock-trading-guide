Stock Market Hours

Regular Market Hours

The regular U.S. stock market is usually open Monday through Friday from 9:30 AM to 4:00 PM Eastern Time.

Pre-Market

Pre-market happens before the regular market opens. Prices can move, but there may be less volume and bigger price swings.

After-Hours

After-hours trading happens after the regular market closes. Beginners should be careful because prices can move quickly with lower volume.

Weekends and Holidays

The stock market is usually closed on weekends and certain holidays. Crypto trades 24/7, but stocks do not.

Beginner Tip

Most beginners should learn during regular market hours first because there is usually more activity and clearer movement.
