Realistic Trading Expectations

Important

Trading is risky. This website is for education only, not financial advice.

Trading Takes Time

Most beginners do not become successful quickly. Trading is a skill that takes practice, studying, patience, and discipline.

You Will Not Win Every Trade

No trader wins 100% of the time. The goal is to manage risk and learn from every trade.

Do Not Try To Get Rich Fast

Rushing, chasing hype, and risking too much can lead to big losses. Focus on learning first.

Practice Before Real Money

Use paper trading to practice reading charts, making plans, and controlling emotions before risking real money.

Beginner Mindset

☐ I will learn first.

☐ I will practice with fake money.

☐ I will not trade bill money.

☐ I will accept losses as part of learning.

☐ I will focus on consistency, not quick money.
