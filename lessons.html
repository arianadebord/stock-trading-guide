Complete Beginner Trading Lessons

Lesson 1: What Is Trading?

Trading means buying and selling stocks, ETFs, or other assets hoping to make a profit. The goal is to buy at one price and sell at a better price.

Lesson 2: Trading vs Investing

Investing usually means holding for months or years. Trading is usually shorter-term and requires more practice, research, and risk control.

Lesson 3: What Is a Broker?

A broker is the app or company you use to buy and sell stocks. Examples include investing apps and online brokerage accounts.

Lesson 4: What Is Paper Trading?

Paper trading is practice trading with fake money. Beginners should practice first before risking real money.

Lesson 5: What Moves Stock Prices?

Stock prices can move because of company news, earnings reports, market trends, interest rates, investor emotions, and world events.
