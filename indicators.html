Beginner Technical Indicators

Important

Indicators do not predict the future. They are tools to help study price movement.

Moving Average

A moving average smooths out price movement. Beginners use it to see the general direction of a trend.

RSI

RSI stands for Relative Strength Index. It helps show if a stock may be overbought or oversold, but it is not always correct.

MACD

MACD is a momentum indicator. Traders use it to study changes in trend strength.

VWAP

VWAP means Volume Weighted Average Price. It shows an average price based on both price and volume.

Bollinger Bands

Bollinger Bands show price movement around an average. They can help show volatility.

Beginner Rule

Do not use too many indicators at once. Start with price, volume, support, resistance, and one or two indicators.
