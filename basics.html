Stock Market Basics

What is a stock?

A stock is a piece of ownership in a company. If the company does well, the stock price may go up. If it does badly, the price may go down.

What is a share?

A share is one unit of stock. For example, if you buy 1 share of Apple, you own a tiny piece of Apple.

What makes prices move?

News, earnings reports, company growth, interest rates, trends, and investor emotions can move stock prices.
