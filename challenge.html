Paper Trading Challenge

Challenge Rules

You start with $10,000 fake money. Your goal is not just profit. Your goal is discipline.

Complete These Goals

Complete 10 fake trades

Write every trade in your journal

Use a trading plan every time

Avoid revenge trading

Practice reading support and resistance

Review your mistakes

Complete 50 fake trades before real money

Challenge Tip

If you cannot follow rules with fake money, real money will be even harder. Practice discipline first.
