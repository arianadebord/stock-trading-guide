Beginner Trading Journal

What Is a Trading Journal?

A trading journal is where you write down every practice trade you make. It helps you learn from your choices instead of guessing.

Why It Matters

A journal helps you notice patterns, mistakes, emotions, and what you need to improve before using real money.

What To Write Down

☐ Stock ticker

☐ Buy price

☐ Sell price

☐ Why you entered the trade

☐ Why you exited the trade

☐ Whether it was a win or loss

☐ What you learned

Example Journal Entry

Ticker: TSLA

Entry: $250

Exit: $255

Reason: Practiced buying after price moved upward.

Lesson: I need to wait for a clear plan before clicking buy.

Beginner Rule

If you cannot explain why you made a trade, you probably should not make it yet.
