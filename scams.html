Trading Scams & Red Flags

Protect Yourself

Trading is risky. Be careful with anyone promising easy money or guaranteed profits.

Guaranteed Profit Claims

If someone says you will “definitely” make money, that is a red flag. No trade is guaranteed.

Paid Signal Groups

Some groups charge money for “buy and sell alerts.” Beginners should be careful because alerts can be wrong, late, or risky.

Social Media Hype

Do not buy a stock just because someone on TikTok, YouTube, Discord, or Instagram says it will go up.

Pump and Dump

This happens when people hype up a stock so others buy it, then they sell and leave beginners with losses.

Beginner Safety Rules

☐ Avoid guaranteed profit promises.

☐ Avoid rushing into trades.

☐ Do your own research.

☐ Practice with fake money first.

☐ Never send money to random trading accounts.
