Beginner Trading FAQ

How much money do I need to start?

You can start learning with $0 by paper trading. Practice first before using real money.

Can I lose money trading?

Yes. Trading can lose money. That is why risk management and practice are important.

Should beginners day trade?

Beginners should be very careful with day trading. It is fast, emotional, and risky. Paper trade first.

What is paper trading?

Paper trading is fake-money practice trading. It helps you learn without risking real money.

What should I learn first?

Start with stock basics, chart reading, risk management, and trading psychology.

What is the most important beginner rule?

Never trade money you cannot afford to lose.
