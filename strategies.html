Beginner Trading Strategies

Important

These are for learning only. No strategy guarantees profit.

Trend Following

This means watching whether a stock is moving upward or downward and learning how trends work before making decisions.

Buy and Hold

This is more investing than trading. It means buying strong companies or funds and holding them for a long time.

Dollar-Cost Averaging

This means investing a set amount regularly instead of putting all your money in at once.

Breakout Watching

A breakout happens when a stock moves above an important price area. Beginners should practice spotting this with fake money first.

Risk-First Strategy

Before every trade, decide how much you are willing to lose. A good trader thinks about risk before profit.
