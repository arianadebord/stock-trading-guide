Stock Screener Guide

What Is a Stock Screener?

A stock screener helps you search for stocks based on filters like price, volume, market cap, sector, and performance.

Price Filter

This lets you search for stocks in a certain price range. Beginners should avoid choosing only because a stock looks cheap.

Volume Filter

Volume shows how many shares are traded. Higher volume can make buying and selling easier.

Market Cap

Market cap means company size. Large-cap companies are usually bigger and more established. Small-cap stocks may move more but can be riskier.

Sector

A sector is a business category like technology, healthcare, energy, financials, or consumer goods.

Beginner Screener Checklist

☐ I know what the company does.

☐ I checked volume.

☐ I checked recent news.

☐ I looked at the chart.

☐ I am not buying just because the price is low.
