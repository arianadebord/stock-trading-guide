Step-by-Step Trading Roadmap

Step 1: Learn What Stocks Are

A stock is a small piece of ownership in a company. Prices move up and down based on demand, news, earnings, and the economy.

Step 2: Learn the Risks

Never trade money you cannot afford to lose. Start small and practice before using real money.

Step 3: Choose a Trading Platform

Common beginner platforms include Robinhood, Fidelity, Webull, Charles Schwab, and E*TRADE.

Step 4: Practice First

Use paper trading to practice without risking real money.

Step 5: Build a Trading Plan

Decide what you will buy, when you will buy, when you will sell, and how much money you are willing to risk.
